Moving Images and Effects

Applications Used: Premiere Pro & After Effects

Concept

My film called “In my world”. My conceptual is to bring the audience into my world. I want to show the audience, as a media maker, how I design the world. I used a door at the beginning of my short film, which means bring the audience into my world. All the shots I took came from scenes that I often see in my daily life, But I will use ingenious editing and special effects to make them consistent and special. I used different transitions to connect every two shots to make my short film consistent. The background music is very important to my short film. My scenes are switched according to the rhythm of the background music.

Process & Final Work

My style is bright and vibrant. I adjusted the tones of each scene to make sure they look natural and comfortable. Through the style and visual effect to explain why I’m a media maker. For example, I used rotation effect connect human’s eye and tyre of the car. I tried to make each of my shots connected and interesting. Make the audience expect the next shot.
